Text: 

This map explores  the risks and rewards of living in the Bay Area.  Modem used custom software tools and a modified IBM Wheelwriter typewriter to imprint aggregated text onto 263 letter-sized pages, juxtaposing quotidien interactions with the density of geological hazards present in the physical landscape.  Eureka was a site-specific installation for the Yerba Buena Center for the Arts' Bay Area Now 8 triennial.

Composite typewriter drawing on paper 
90 x 264 inches

With Jaclyn Berry, Jeremy Ferguson, <a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="http://ecosimulation.com/chrisgregg/">Chris Gregg</a>, and Carlos Sandoval
